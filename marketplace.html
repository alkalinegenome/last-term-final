Citrusy & Clean

Every bean in Guatemala El Paraiso is grown on the Martinez family farm, the first farm encouraged by Caribou Coffee to achieve Rainforest Alliance certification. We're proud to call them our friends, and proud to bring you this 100% Rainforest Alliance Certified coffee.

Guatemala El Paraiso has the hallmark citrus and floral flavors of the Huehuetenango Region, along with cranberry and dried cherry notes.

  • Fresh floral flavors
  • Rainforest Alliance Certified

Sumatra Samosir Batak

Vibrant & Earthly

We partnered with the most quality-renowned exporter from Sumatra to create this special coffee. This exotic coffee is grown and processed by the Batak people who live in the coffee production areas around Lake Toba. This is a bio-diverse area that is home to 201 mammal species, 580 bird species and the largest flowers on earth.

Tasting Notes:

Flavors of sweet pipe tobacco, cedar wood spice, juicy apricot and vanilla all intertwine with a heavy herbal note and deliver an impressive weight in the cup. These flavors are the result of the unique processing used in Sumatra - a sort of hybrid method between the wet and dry techniques for removing the beans from cherries.

Roastmaster's Notes:

  • Woody, aromatic, medium-dark roast
  • Hints of tobacco and apricot
  • Vibrant herbal notes
  • Highly energizing roast

Sumatra is a 100% Rainforest Alliance Certified® coffee.

Mocha Java

Spicy & Deep

While many coffee shops sell Mocha Java, very few contain the real thing: Yemen Mocha Mattari. This rare bean has been grown and dry-processed the same way for hundreds of years - and it makes up a full 40 percent of our Mocha Java. To perfectly balance and elicit the legendary Mocha qualities, we blend it with Java, Costa Rica, and Ethiopia Harrar.

Tasting Notes:

This is a robust, spicy, intensely fruity blend with hints of bittersweet chocolate, blueberries and wild honey in a big, bold body. Due to the heavy percentage of naturally processed coffee in this blend, it packs a level of sweetness and fruitiness not present in many other blends.

Roastmaster's Notes:

  • Rich, spicy, medium-dark roast
  • Wild honey high notes
  • Chocolate mousse body and sweetness
  • Memorable, relaxing blend

Mocha Java is a 100% Rainforest Alliance Certified® coffee.

Cross Fox Espresso

Creamy & Indulgent

It is important to note that espresso is a brewing method. If you like the taste of our Cross Fox Espresso Blend, use any brewing method you like to brew this terrific coffee! Our Cross Fox Espresso Blend produces a cup that is sweet, smooth and sophisticated with a powerful flavor and a hint of berry that explodes in your mouth.

Tasting Notes:

Our Cross Fox Espresso Blend has the bitterness to punch through milk, making it ideal for a latte or cappuccino, or exquisite on its own. This blend has a lot of body, and produces a cup that is sweet, smooth, and sophisticated. Expect caramel and chocolate sweetness, followed by a satisfying, long lasting, syrupy finish.

Roastmaster's Notes:

  • Pungent, dark roast
  • Hints of berry
  • Sweet, syrupy caramel finish
  • Try french pressed or drip-brewed

Cross Fox Espresso Blend is a 100% Rainforest Alliance Certified® coffee
