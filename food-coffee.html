Espresso

Smooth and bold, our Cross Fox espresso is the beating heart of Caribou Coffee. Sweet and sophisticated, it's powerful enough to be the perfect base for our lattes, cappuccinos and other espresso-based beverages. However, with hints of berry and chocolate with a syrupy caramel finish, it's exquisite on its own.
